Penn Clubs provides comprehensive tools for club officers to manage their organizations, members, and online presence.

## Club Profile Management

Club officers can edit and maintain their club's profile through the management dashboard.

### Basic Information

Edit core club details:

- **Club name** and subtitle
- **Description** with rich text formatting
- **Founded date** and club history
- **Size** category (1-20, 21-50, 51-100, 101+ members)
- **Contact email** and visibility settings
- **Physical address** (if applicable)

### Branding and Media

Customize your club's visual identity:

<Card title="Club Logo" icon="image">
  Upload a logo that appears in search results and club pages. Supports PNG, JPG, and SVG formats.
</Card>

<Card title="Thumbnail Generation" icon="crop">
  System automatically creates optimized thumbnails for faster loading
</Card>

<Card title="Social Media Links" icon="share-2">
  Connect Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, GitHub, and YouTube accounts
</Card>

### Categories and Tags

Organize your club's identity:

- **Primary Category** - Main classification (Academic, Cultural, Social, etc.)
- **Tags** - Multiple tags to improve discoverability
- **Badges** - Special designations (Wharton Council, SAC Funded, etc.)
- **Target Audience** - Specify years, schools, and majors

## Member Management

Comprehensive member management with role-based permissions.

### Membership Roles

<Steps>
  <Step title="Owner">
    Full control over club settings, members, and all features. Can promote/demote other members.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Officer">
    Manage events, applications, and most club features. Cannot modify owner permissions.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Member">
    Basic membership with access to member-only content and events.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing Members

Club officers can:

- **View all members** with filtering by role (Owner, Officer, Member)
- **Edit member roles** and titles
- **Mark members as active or inactive** (for alumni tracking)
- **Remove members** from the club (with confirmation)
- **Export member list** to Excel for external use
- **Set member visibility** (public or private profiles)

### Member Invitations

Invite new members to your club:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Invite">
    Enter the email address and select the role (Member, Officer, or Owner)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send Email">
    System automatically sends an invitation email with a secure link
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Member Accepts">
    Recipient clicks the link and is automatically added to the club
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Expiration">
    Optionally set an expiration date for invitations
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Invitation links are secured with unique tokens and can only be used once.
</Note>

### Membership Requests

Handle requests from students who want to join:

- **View pending requests** with requester information
- **Approve or deny** requests with one click
- **Automatic notifications** to requesters
- **Withdrawn requests** tracking

### Ownership Transfers

Transfer ownership of a club:

- Current owners receive **ownership requests** from other members
- **Accept or deny** requests with proper verification
- **6-month request cooldown** to prevent spam
- **Status tracking** (Pending, Accepted, Denied, Withdrawn)

## Club Settings

### Approval and Registration

Manage your club's official status:

- **Approval status** - Track OSA approval for official recognition
- **Registration renewal** - Submit annual renewal applications
- **Approval queue** - View position in approval pipeline
- **Comments and feedback** from administrators

### Recruitment Settings

Configure how your club recruits new members:

- **Accepting members** toggle
- **Application requirements** (Open, Audition, Tryout, Application, Application + Interview)
- **Recruiting cycle** (Fall, Spring, Both, Open, Unknown)
- **Target audience** specifications

### Privacy Settings

<Card title="Public Visibility" icon="eye">
  Control whether your club is visible to unauthenticated users
</Card>

<Card title="Email Visibility" icon="mail">
  Choose whether to display contact email publicly
</Card>

<Card title="Subscriptions" icon="bell">
  Enable or disable subscription functionality for your club
</Card>

## Advisors and Points of Contact

Manage faculty advisors and administrators:

- **Add advisors** with name, title, department, email, and phone
- **Set visibility** (Admin Only, Signed-in Students, Public)
- **Multiple advisors** supported per club

## Notes and Communication

### Internal Notes

For clubs with parent organizations:

- **Create notes** about constituent clubs
- **Tag notes** for categorization
- **Set permissions** for who can view notes
- **Track note history** and authors

### Admin Notes

Administrators can:

- **Add administrative notes** visible to club officers
- **Track club interactions** and issues
- **Maintain records** for approval processes

## Analytics and Reporting

Club officers can view analytics about their club:

- **Page visit statistics**
- **Favorite/bookmark counts**
- **Member growth tracking**
- **Event attendance data**
- **Fair participation metrics**

<Note>
  Analytics help clubs understand their reach and engagement with the Penn community.
</Note>

## Files and Assets

Upload and manage club files:

- **Documents** for members (constitutions, bylaws, etc.)
- **Images** for promotional use
- **File permissions** and access control
- **Version tracking** for important documents

## Club Fair Registration

Register for activities fairs:

- **Browse upcoming fairs** (SAC Fair, etc.)
- **Submit registration** with fair-specific questions
- **Manage fair events** and booth information
- **Receive fair reminders** via email

## QR Codes

Generate QR codes for your club:

- **Instant QR code generation** linking to club page
- **Download QR codes** for printing on flyers
- **Share links** to club profile

<Note>
  QR codes are perfect for tabling at fairs or posting on campus flyers.
</Note>
