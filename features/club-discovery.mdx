Penn Clubs provides a comprehensive discovery platform that helps students find clubs that match their interests, schedule, and goals.

## Search and Browse

The club discovery system allows students to explore Penn's vibrant student organization ecosystem through multiple pathways.

### Text Search

Students can search for clubs by name, description, or keywords. The search bar provides:

- **Real-time search** with instant results as you type
- **Smart matching** that finds relevant clubs based on descriptions and tags
- **Clear search** functionality to quickly reset and start a new search

The search automatically saves query history and can be used to quickly find previously viewed clubs.

### Advanced Filtering

Penn Clubs offers multiple filter categories to narrow down club results:

<Card title="Filter by Category" icon="tag">
  Filter clubs by their primary category such as Academic, Cultural, Social, Pre-Professional, or Community Service
</Card>

<Card title="Filter by Tags" icon="bookmark">
  Refine results using specific tags like "Technology," "Business," "Arts," or "Athletics"
</Card>

<Card title="Filter by Size" icon="users">
  Find clubs based on membership size: Small (1-20), Medium (21-50), Large (51-100), or Very Large (101+)
</Card>

<Card title="Filter by Application Type" icon="file-text">
  Filter by how clubs accept members: Open Membership, Audition Required, Tryout Required, Application Required, or Application and Interview Required
</Card>

### Sorting Options

Clubs can be sorted by different criteria:

- **Featured** - Default ranking based on club activity, membership, and engagement
- **Name** - Alphabetical ordering
- **Size** - By membership count
- **Recently Updated** - Clubs that have recently updated their information

## Club Listings

Each club appears in the search results with key information:

- **Club logo** and branding
- **Club name** and subtitle
- **Tags** indicating interests and categories
- **Active status** indicator
- **Membership information**

### Club Pages

Clicking on any club opens its dedicated page with comprehensive information:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Overview">
    View the club's mission, description, and how to get involved
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Events">
    See upcoming events and meetings hosted by the club
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Membership">
    Learn about membership requirements and current officers
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Resources">
    Access links to social media, website, and contact information
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Favorites and Bookmarks

Students can bookmark clubs they're interested in:

- **Favorite clubs** to save them for later review
- **Subscribe** to receive updates and notifications from clubs
- **View all favorites** in one place from your profile

<Note>
  Favoriting a club helps you keep track of organizations you're interested in and allows clubs to see student interest levels.
</Note>

## School and Major Targeting

Clubs can specify target audiences based on:

- **Year** (Freshman, Sophomore, Junior, Senior, Graduate)
- **School** (Wharton, Engineering, College, Nursing, etc.)
- **Major** (specific academic programs)
- **Student Type** (International Students, Transfer Students, etc.)

Students can filter clubs based on these criteria to find the most relevant organizations.

## Mobile Experience

The club discovery interface is fully responsive:

- **Mobile filters** collapse into a menu for easy access
- **Touch-friendly** club cards and navigation
- **Fast loading** with optimized images
- **Saved searches** persist across devices

## Public vs. Authenticated Views

Penn Clubs offers different experiences based on authentication:

- **Public view** - Basic club information visible to anyone
- **Authenticated view** - Full access to club details, contact information, and member features
- **Member view** - Additional features for club members and officers

<Note>
  Some clubs choose to remain visible only to authenticated Penn students for privacy and community building.
</Note>
