Penn Clubs is your central hub for discovering student organizations, joining clubs, and participating in campus activities at the University of Pennsylvania. This guide will help you make the most of the platform as a student.

## Discovering Clubs

### Searching and Filtering

The Penn Clubs homepage features a powerful search system to help you find organizations that match your interests.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Browse Page">
    Navigate to the Browse page to see all available student organizations.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use the Search Bar">
    Enter keywords in the search bar to find clubs by name, description, or tags. Results update automatically as you type.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply Filters">
    Refine your search using filters:
    - **Tags**: Filter by interest areas (e.g., Arts, Sports, Technology)
    - **Affiliations**: View clubs with specific institutional affiliations
    - **Size**: Find clubs by membership size (under 20, 20-50, 50-100, 100+)
    - **Membership Process**: Filter by application requirements (Open, Tryout, Audition, Application)
    - **Recruiting Cycle**: See when clubs are actively recruiting (Fall, Spring, Year-round)
    - **Student Level**: Filter by undergraduate or graduate level
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Sort Results">
    Sort clubs by:
    - Featured (default)
    - Alphabetical order
    - Most bookmarked
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Viewing Club Pages

Each club has a dedicated page with detailed information:

- **Club description** and mission statement
- **Contact information** (email, website, social media)
- **Membership details** (size, application requirements, recruiting cycle)
- **Events calendar** showing upcoming activities
- **FAQ section** with questions from other students
- **Members list** showing current officers and members
- **How to get involved** section with participation details

## Bookmarking and Subscribing

### Bookmarking Clubs

Bookmark clubs you're interested in to save them for later.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Bookmark from Club Page">
    Click the bookmark (heart) icon on any club page to add it to your favorites.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Your Bookmarks">
    Access all your bookmarked clubs from the Settings page under the "Bookmarks" tab.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Remove Bookmarks">
    Click the bookmark icon again to remove a club from your favorites.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Subscribing to Clubs

Subscribe to clubs to receive updates about their activities and events.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Subscribe from Club Page">
    Click the "Subscribe" button on a club page to receive notifications.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manage Subscriptions">
    View all your subscriptions in Settings under the "Subscriptions" tab.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Unsubscribe">
    Remove subscriptions by clicking "Unsubscribe" on the club page or from your Subscriptions list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Joining Clubs

### Requesting Membership

For clubs with open membership:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Club Page">
    Navigate to the page of the club you want to join.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Request Membership">
    If the club has open membership and is accepting new members, click the "Request Membership" button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Approval">
    Club officers will review your request. You'll receive a notification when your membership is approved.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track Your Requests">
    View pending membership requests in Settings under the "Requests" tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Applying to Clubs

For clubs that require applications:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Application Status">
    On the club page, check if applications are currently open and note any deadlines.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Apply">
    Click the "Apply" button on the club page to access the application.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Requirements">
    Read the application requirements carefully, including:
    - Application type (written application, interview, audition, tryout)
    - Recruiting cycle (when they recruit new members)
    - Any specific eligibility requirements
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Application">
    Complete and submit your application through the provided external link or platform.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track Your Submission">
    Monitor your application status in Settings under the "Submissions" tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Asking Questions

The FAQ feature allows you to ask questions directly to club officers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to FAQ Section">
    Scroll to the FAQ section on any club page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Existing Questions">
    Review questions other students have asked. You can:
    - Sort by most recent or most liked
    - Like helpful answers
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Ask a New Question">
    Click "Ask a Question" to submit your own question to club officers.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Get Notified">
    You'll receive a notification when club officers respond to your question.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Attending Events

### Finding Events

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Events Page">
    Navigate to the Events page to see all upcoming activities.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse by Date">
    Filter events by date range to find activities that fit your schedule.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Event Details">
    Click any event to see:
    - Date, time, and location
    - Event description
    - Host club information
    - Ticket availability (if applicable)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Event Tickets

For ticketed events:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register for Tickets">
    Click "Get Tickets" on the event page when tickets become available.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Registration">
    Fill out any required information and submit your ticket request.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Your Tickets">
    Access your tickets in Settings under the "Tickets" tab.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Transfer or Cancel">
    If needed, you can transfer tickets to other students or cancel your registration (if the event allows it).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Your Profile

Keep your profile updated to receive relevant club recommendations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Settings">
    Click on your profile icon and select "Settings."
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Profile Information">
    Under the "Profile" tab, update:
    - Your school and major
    - Graduation year
    - Student type
    - Interests and preferences
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manage Notifications">
    Configure how you want to receive updates from clubs and the platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Participating in Club Fairs

Penn Clubs hosts virtual and in-person club fairs throughout the year.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Fair Page">
    Navigate to the Club Fair page when a fair is active.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Participating Clubs">
    Explore clubs registered for the fair, often organized by category.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Join Virtual Meetings">
    For virtual fairs, click on club Zoom links to join live information sessions.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Subscribe During the Fair">
    Easily bookmark or subscribe to clubs directly from the fair interface.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="How do I know if a club is accepting new members?">
  Check the club page for an "Accepting Members" indicator. Clubs can mark whether they're currently recruiting, and you can filter for clubs accepting members on the Browse page.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What's the difference between bookmarking and subscribing?">
  Bookmarking saves a club to your favorites list for easy access. Subscribing means you'll receive updates and notifications about the club's activities and events.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I see which clubs my friends are in?">
  Yes! Visit a friend's profile to see their club memberships (if they've made this information public).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I find clubs specific to my school or major?">
  Use the "School Specific" and "Student Type" filters on the Browse page to find clubs targeted to your academic background.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What does 'ghost club' or 'pending approval' mean?">
  This indicates the club has recent changes awaiting approval from the university administration. The club page may show pending updates.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I contact a club if they don't respond to my question?">
  Visit the club page and use the contact information in the "Contact" section (email, social media, or website) to reach out directly.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I request ownership of an inactive club?">
  Yes! If you find an inactive club you'd like to revive, click the "Request Ownership" button on the club page (if available).
</Accordion>

## Getting Help

If you encounter any issues or have questions not covered in this guide:

- Check the FAQ section on individual club pages
- Contact the club directly using their provided contact information
- Reach out to Penn Clubs support for technical issues with the platform
- Visit the main Penn Clubs page for additional resources and documentation
